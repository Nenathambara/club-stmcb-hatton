Inventor's Club

St. Mary's College, Bogawanthalawa

Welcome to the Inventor's Club of St. Mary's College, Bogawanthalawa! Established on the 8th of November 2024, this club aims to ignite the passion for innovation and technology among students. Focused on using Arduino and other electronics platforms, we encourage hands-on learning, creative problem-solving, and the development of practical solutions for real-world challenges. Together, we aspire to inspire the next generation of inventors and engineers.

Project Ideas

We will learn to implement the following projects using Tinkercad simulator:

  1. Water Level Sensor: Activates a motor when the water level is low, shows level using LEDs, and rings a buzzer when full.
  2. Gas Leak Detector: Rings a buzzer and lights an indicator during gas leakage.
  3. Automated Room Lighting: Uses an IR sensor to control lights.
  4. Electronic Ceremonial Lamp: Lights 20 LEDs remotely via WiFi.
  5. Automated Plant Watering: Measures soil moisture and activates a pump.
